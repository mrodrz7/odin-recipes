Chorizo Tacos

Description

Taco Tuesday anyone? What better way to end your day than with an at-home recipe for some awesome Chorizo tacos?!

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizon sausage link
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste

How to Make

  1. Combine crumbled chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce in a bowl
  2. Heat a skiller over medium-high heat; add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Transfer to a plate, reserving grease in the skillet
  4. Heat tortillas in reserved grease in the skiller over medium heat until warmed, 1 to 2 minutes per side
  5. Stack 2 tortillas for each taco, then fill with chorizo, onion, and cilantro
