How to make Macaroni & Cheese

Simple Mac & Cheese

Out of lunch options? Make your own! C'mon, who doesn't love macaroni & cheese?

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8 oz) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil large pot of slightly salted water - cook elbow macaroni - 8 minutes. Stir.
  2. Simultaneously melt butter in sauce pan over medium heat
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth - 5 minutes
  4. Pour milk in slowly and stir - 5 minutes until smooth and bubbling
  5. Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted - 2-4 minutes
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until fully coated
  7. Serve hot and enjoy
