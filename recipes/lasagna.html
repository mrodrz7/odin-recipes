How to make Lasagna

Quick and Easy Lasagna

This is a great and quick recipe for a busy parent trying to feed a hungry family after a long day!

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 1 hr 10 mins
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef or Ground Turkey
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses (cottage, mozzarella, parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings (dried parsley, salt, black pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water (1/2 cup water around edges of baking dish before baking)

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain ground beef/turkey, then stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble lasagna according to detailed recipe
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 min.
