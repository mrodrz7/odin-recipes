How to make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies

Some fresh baked chocolate chip cookies on a lazy Sunday? Sign us up!

  • Prep Time: 20 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Servings: 48

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Beat butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown
